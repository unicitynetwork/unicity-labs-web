Join the Unicity Network and contribute to its security and decentralization by running your own node.

All you need is a CPU

Built on an ASIC-resistant Proof-of-Work (PoW) algorithm, Alpha Miner ensures fair participation by preventing specialized hardware from dominating mining. Anyone with a standard CPU can contribute and earn rewards while securing the network.
